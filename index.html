About Me

Hello & welcome! My name is Rohan Natraj. I am a user experience researcher and product developer. I believe beautiful products are created by understanding the needs and wants of one's audience. CareerTrends

CareerTrends is a website that provides a wealth of information about salaries, employment and even personality types to help job seekers make informed career moves. This project inolved extensive use of MySQL and ETL (using Pentaho Kettle) to structure & analyze several datasets from government sources. Launching this website required cross-disciplinary collaboration with individuals from engineering and business development.

DocNote

DocNote is a clean and simple way to help NeuroRadiology students annotate, save, share and study the many images they diagnose on a daily basis. After several months of working with the students and faculty at Tufts Medical Center, DocNote was designed and prototyped based on the needs and goals of the department. This project involved contextual interviewing, observation, workflow analysis, storyboarding, user persona creation and usability testing.
